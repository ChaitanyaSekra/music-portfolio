Who is Sekra OG
Raw Bars.
Real Words.

SEKRA OG is a rapper and music producer out of Jaipur, Rajasthan — crafting verses that sit at the intersection of street realism, desi identity, and raw lyricism. The music moves between Hindi boom-bap, introspective freestyles, and lo-fi hip-hop — always authentic, always unfiltered.

Off the mic, a Computer Science graduate with a deep focus in AI/ML and web dev — proving that bars and builds aren't as different as people think.
